About Us – Sure 60 Gurukul

Empowering Aspirants for a Brighter Future

Founded in 2017 by Gaurav Kumar Singh , Sure 60 Gurukul was born from a singular, powerful mission: to bridge the gap for students who lack the resources and guidance required to conquer India’s most competitive government examinations. We believe that with the right environment and mentorship, every dedicated aspirant can achieve their dream of a government career.

Our Story: From Struggle to Success

Many students migrate to major hubs like Delhi, only to find themselves lost in crowded classrooms without personalized support. At Sure 60 Gurukul, we saw this struggle firsthand. We don’t just teach; we provide a complete, one-year residential program designed to keep students focused, disciplined, and exam-ready from day one. We take the guesswork out of preparation, showing you exactly how to study and what to prioritize to ensure your selection.

Why Choose Sure 60 Gurukul?

We stand by the principle of equal education for all . Our residential campus in Karnal is built to foster talent over background. Integrated Preparation: We cover all stages (Prelims, Mains, and Descriptive) simultaneously, ensuring comprehensive subject mastery. Structured Residential Program: A disciplined, library-focused environment that removes daily life distractions. Expert Mentorship: Learn from seasoned faculty who specialize in SSC, Railways, and Defence exams. Strategic Study Plans: We provide vetted, well-researched study materials that align with the latest exam patterns. 24/7 Support: Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions to ensure no student is left behind.

Our Vision

We envision an India where quality education is accessible and fair. Sure 60 Gurukul is our contribution toward creating a merit-based path to success for every hardworking student across the nation.
